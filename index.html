How it works

How do I pay with crypto?

At checkout, pick a coin (BTC, USDT, USDC, or SOL). We show a wallet address and a QR code. Send the exact USD value in that coin from your own wallet or exchange, then click “I've sent payment.” You'll get a reference number instantly.

Which coins and networks do you accept?

Bitcoin (BTC), Tether (USDT, ERC-20), USD Coin (USDC, ERC-20), and Solana (SOL). Always send USDT and USDC on the ERC-20 network — sending on the wrong network can lose your funds.

What happens after I pay?

We confirm the transaction on-chain (usually within a few hours), then email a tracking number to the address you provide. Nothing ships until payment is verified.

Is everything authentic?

Yes. Graded slabs come sealed from PSA or CGC. Sealed product is verified case-fresh — never searched, weighed, or resealed. High-value items ship fully insured and signature-required.

How is my order shipped?

Fully insured, tracked, and discreetly packaged from a smoke-free, climate-controlled vault. High-value slabs require a signature on delivery.

Can I get a refund?

Because crypto payments are final, we don't offer change-of-mind refunds. If an item arrives damaged or not as described, contact us and we'll make it right.
